About Us

Kinamba Farm is an agricultural company located in the heart of Kenya. We are a producer of global GAP certified apples and fresh herbs ( rosemary,lemongrass,thyme,basil among others.) We are located in Ndindika Laikipia County with 300 acres of farming.
